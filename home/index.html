ResidentAI Chief of Staff

ResidentAI Chief of Staff is a privately operated, single-user AI assistant. It is not a commercial product and is not offered to the public — it has exactly one user: its operator, who is also its developer. It runs entirely on infrastructure the operator owns and controls.

What it does

ResidentAI Chief of Staff helps its operator manage personal correspondence and scheduling. With approved Google API access, it can:

  • Read the operator's Gmail messages, to answer questions about their own email.
  • Create draft email messages in the operator's Gmail account, for the operator to review.
  • Send a draft email — but only a specific draft the operator has personally reviewed and explicitly approved in that exact form. It never sends email autonomously or without the operator's direct, explicit approval of that exact message.
  • Read events on the operator's Google Calendar, to answer questions about their own schedule.
  • Create new events on the operator's Google Calendar, at the operator's direction.
Every action happens only at the operator's direction, under governed approval. This assistant does not act on its own initiative. Every capability above is invoked by the operator's own request, and anything that changes the operator's account (creating a draft, sending an approved email, adding a calendar event) is gated on the operator's explicit, in-the-moment approval.

How your data is handled

All processing happens on private server infrastructure owned and administered solely by the operator, using a locally-hosted language model. Google user data accessed by this application:

  • Is processed locally — it is not transmitted to or processed by any third-party service, cloud provider, or external company beyond Google's own APIs.
  • Is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for any purpose, including advertising.
  • Is never used to train any generalized or third-party machine learning model.

Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
